1. Overview
------------
Godavari Institute of Engineering and Technology (GIET) is a leading engineering college in Rajahmundry, Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 1998, GIET has grown into a center of excellence for technical education, research, and holistic student development. The institution is affiliated with JNTU Kakinada and holds NAAC A+ and NBA accreditations, reflecting its commitment to quality.

2. History & Vision
-------------------
GIET was founded with the vision to empower students with world-class engineering education and foster innovation. Over the years, GIET has evolved, adapting to technological advancements and industry needs. The college emphasizes ethical values, leadership, and lifelong learning.

ADMISSION PROCESS & PLACEMENTS

A. Admission Process
-------------------
- UG Admissions (B.Tech):
  - Entrance Exams: AP EAMCET, JEE Main
  - Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, minimum 45% marks
  - Application: Online/offline, counseling by APSCHE
  - Seat Allocation: 70% through entrance rank, 30% merit/NRI/other states
  - Document Verification: Marksheet, entrance scorecard, ID, transfer certificate, caste/income certificate, photos
  - Counseling Rounds: Preference selection, seat allotment, fee payment
  - Orientation: Academic calendar, campus tour, student handbook

- PG Admissions (M.Tech, MBA, MCA):
  - Entrance Exams: GATE, AP PGECET (M.Tech), APICET (MBA/MCA)
  - Eligibility: Graduation with minimum 50% marks
  - Application: Online registration, counseling, document verification
  - Seat Allocation: Based on entrance score, merit
  - Final Admission: Fee payment, registration

- Diploma & Ph.D. Admissions:
  - Diploma: 10th pass, entrance/merit
  - Ph.D.: Master’s degree, research proposal, interview
